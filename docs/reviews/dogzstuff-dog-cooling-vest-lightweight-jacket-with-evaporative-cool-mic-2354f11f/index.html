Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right size for my dog?

Measure your dog's chest and neck circumference, then refer to the sizing chart provided by DOGZSTUFF. Ensure you select a size that allows for a snug but comfortable fit.

How do I activate the cooling effect?

Soak the vest in cold water, wring out the excess, and then place it on your dog. The evaporative cooling technology will help keep your pet cool.

Is the vest safe for all-day wear?

Yes, the vest is designed for extended use. However, always monitor your dog for any signs of discomfort and adjust the fit as needed.

How should I clean the vest?

Hand wash the vest with mild detergent and rinse thoroughly. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, and let it air dry completely before storing.

Can the vest be used indoors?

While primarily designed for outdoor use, the vest can also be beneficial indoors if your home is warm. Ensure your dog is comfortable and not too cool.
