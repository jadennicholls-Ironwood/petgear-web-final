What kind of maintenance does the reservoir and nozzle need?

Empty the tank after extended use and allow it to air dry to reduce mineral buildup when using hard water. Allow treated surfaces to cool before permitting pets back on them, and ventilate the room if you are cleaning pet bedding to reduce lingering steam and odors.
