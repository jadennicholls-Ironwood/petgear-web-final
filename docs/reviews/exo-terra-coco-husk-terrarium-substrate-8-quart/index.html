Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Exo Terra Coconut Fiber Substrate suitable for all types of tropical lizards?

Yes, this substrate is versatile and can be used for a variety of tropical lizards. Its moisture-retaining properties make it ideal for species that thrive in humid environments.

How often should the substrate be replaced?

It's recommended to replace the substrate every 4-6 weeks or sooner if it becomes overly soiled. Regular spot cleaning can extend its usability.

Can this substrate be used with heating pads?

Yes, it can be used with heating pads. However, ensure the substrate remains moist to prevent it from drying out and becoming a fire hazard.

How do I prepare the substrate before use?

Soak the substrate in water to allow it to expand and absorb moisture. Once fully saturated, drain excess water and spread it evenly in the terrarium.

Is the substrate safe if ingested by pets?

While generally safe, it's best to monitor your pets to prevent excessive ingestion. Ensure the substrate is damp to reduce dust and potential respiratory issues.
