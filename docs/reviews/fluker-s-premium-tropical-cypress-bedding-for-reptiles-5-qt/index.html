Frequently Asked Questions

Is Fluker's Cypress & Forest Mulch suitable for all reptile species?

This mulch is best suited for tropical species like snakes and geckos that require higher humidity levels. It's not ideal for reptiles from arid environments.

How often should I replace the mulch?

Spot cleaning should be done regularly, with a full replacement recommended every 4-6 weeks to maintain optimal conditions.

Can this mulch be used with under-tank heaters?

Yes, but ensure the mulch layer is not too thick to prevent overheating. Regularly monitor the temperature to ensure safety.

Does the mulch produce dust?

There may be some dust during initial setup, but it settles quickly. Lightly misting the mulch can help reduce dust.

Is the mulch safe for burrowing reptiles?

Yes, the mulch is safe and provides a natural substrate for burrowing behaviors, enhancing the habitat's realism.
