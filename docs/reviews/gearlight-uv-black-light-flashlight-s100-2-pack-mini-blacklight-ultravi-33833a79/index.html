How effective are GearLight UV-Free Night Viewing Lights at finding pet urine?

They are effective for highlighting many common pet urine stains on carpets and fabrics because organic compounds fluoresce under the lamp. Results vary with fabric type and how old the stain is, so pair the light with a slow, systematic scan to catch faint spots.

What power source do these pen lights use and how long do they last?

The lights use standard AAA batteries, which are easy to find and swap out when output drops. Battery life depends on usage length and battery quality, but under normal inspection routines expect multiple short sessions per battery set.

Are these safe to use around pets and people during inspections?

Yes, when used as directed they are safe for brief inspections because they emit focused near-UV light rather than high-intensity UV. Avoid staring directly into the beam and do not shine it into anyone's eyes to prevent discomfort.

How should I clean and store the pen light for best longevity?

Wipe the lens and body with a soft cloth and mild cleaner, avoid abrasive materials that scratch the lens, and remove batteries for long-term storage to prevent corrosion. Keep the light in a dry place and use the pocket clip or a small case to prevent knocks.

Can these lights detect bed bugs or other pests reliably?

They can help reveal some traces associated with bed bugs like certain stains and shed skins, but they do not guarantee detection of all pests or all life stages. Use the lights as a quick diagnostic tool alongside visual inspection and other indicators for a fuller assessment.
