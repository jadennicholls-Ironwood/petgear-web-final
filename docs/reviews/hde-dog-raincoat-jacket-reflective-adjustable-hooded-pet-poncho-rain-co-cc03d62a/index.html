Frequently Asked Questions

How do I determine the right size for my dog?

Measure your dog's chest and neck circumference, as well as their back length, to find the best fit. Refer to the sizing chart provided by HDE Dog Raincoats to ensure a comfortable fit.

Is the raincoat suitable for heavy rain?

The raincoat is designed for light to moderate rain. For heavy rain, additional protection may be needed to keep your dog completely dry.

How do I clean the raincoat?

The raincoat can be wiped clean with a damp cloth or hand washed with mild detergent. Allow it to air dry completely before storing.

Can the raincoat be used at night?

Yes, the reflective strips on the raincoat enhance visibility, making it suitable for nighttime walks. Always ensure your dog is visible to vehicles and other pedestrians.

Is the raincoat easy to put on and take off?

Yes, the raincoat features adjustable straps and a simple design, making it easy to put on and remove. This convenience is ideal for quick changes during unpredictable weather.
