Frequently Asked Questions

Is Homey Pet Multi-Level Metal Cages suitable for both chinchillas and ferrets?

Yes, the tiered design accommodates both chinchillas and ferrets by offering vertical space and multiple platforms for movement. Owners should check platform spacing and bar gaps to ensure small feet cannot slip through, and add solid platform covers or mats if needed to protect sensitive paws.

How difficult is assembly for the cage, and what tools are needed?

Assembly is generally straightforward and requires only basic tools like a screwdriver or pliers, which are often supplied or easily available. The modular panels snap or bolt together in clear steps, so a single person can usually complete setup in under an hour with minimal frustration.

What is the best way to clean and maintain this metal cage?

Remove the slide-out trays and dispose of soiled bedding before wiping surfaces with a mild, pet-safe cleaner. Deep clean monthly by disassembling removable components, scrubbing with warm water and mild soap, rinsing thoroughly, and allowing everything to dry completely to prevent rust and lingering odors.

Are there safety tips for using the multi-level layout with active pets?

Ensure ramps and platforms are secure and have non-slip surfaces to prevent falls, and keep bedding depth consistent to cushion impacts. Regularly check latches and panel connections for wear, and avoid placing the cage near open windows or direct drafts that could stress small animals.

Can the cage be moved easily between rooms or stored when not in use?

The cage is portable enough to be moved when empty, especially if panels are detached for transport, which reduces strain and avoids damage. For storage, disassemble panels and stack them flat to save space, and keep small hardware in a labeled bag to make future reassembly simple.
