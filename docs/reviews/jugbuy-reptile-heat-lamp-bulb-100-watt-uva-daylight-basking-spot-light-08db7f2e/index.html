Frequently Asked Questions

Will the Jugbuy bulbs fit a standard clamp lamp?

Most models use a standard medium base and will fit common clamp lamps and simple ceramic fixtures. Always verify the fixture’s maximum wattage rating before use, and position the lamp so the bulb is not touching any plastic or substrate to avoid heat damage.

How often should I replace these basking bulbs?

Bulb life varies with daily run time, but expect to check performance every few months for reduced brightness or uneven heat. Replacing on a schedule helps keep basking temperatures steady and prevents sudden drops that can affect behavior or digestion.

Can these bulbs be used for turtles and amphibians?

Yes, the daylight and heat output suit many reptiles and some semi-aquatic turtles that need a warm basking area. For amphibians, ensure the species tolerates dryer, warmer basking spots and always provide shaded, cooler areas to prevent dehydration.

What cleaning steps keep the bulbs safe and effective?

Allow the bulb to cool fully before cleaning, then gently wipe the glass with a soft cloth and mild cleaner if needed. Avoid abrasive materials and never submerge the bulb in water, and inspect the base for corrosion during routine maintenance.

Are there safety tips for mounting and monitoring these bulbs?

Secure the fixture to prevent falls, keep any flammable materials away, and maintain a safe distance between the bulb and PVC or plastic mesh. Use a thermometer to check the basking spot and consider a guard or reflector to protect animals from direct contact with the hot surface.
