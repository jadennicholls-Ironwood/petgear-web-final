Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install the KENNELS & KATS Window Bird TV Feeders?

Installation is straightforward. Simply clean your window, attach the suction cups to the feeder, and press it firmly against the glass. Ensure it is secure before adding birdseed.

What type of birds can I expect to see?

The feeders attract a variety of small to medium-sized birds, such as finches, sparrows, and chickadees. The type of birdseed used can influence which birds visit.

How do I clean the feeders?

Cleaning is simple. Remove the feeder from the window, empty any remaining seed, and wash with mild soap and water. Rinse thoroughly and dry before reattaching.

Are the feeders safe for birds?

Yes, the feeders are designed with bird safety in mind. They provide a stable feeding platform and are made from non-toxic materials.

Can the feeders withstand harsh weather?

While the feeders are durable, they are best suited for mild weather conditions. In extreme weather, it's advisable to remove and store them indoors to prolong their lifespan.
