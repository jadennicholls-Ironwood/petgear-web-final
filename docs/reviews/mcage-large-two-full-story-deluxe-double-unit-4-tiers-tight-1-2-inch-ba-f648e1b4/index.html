How hard is setup for Mcage Multi-Level Metal Cages?

What cleaning routine keeps the cage hygienic?

Pull-out trays should be emptied and wiped at least weekly, with deeper cleaning once a month using pet-safe cleaners. Wipe bars and platforms regularly to prevent buildup, and replace bedding more often for particularly messy animals.

Are the bar spacing and materials safe for small birds and rodents?

Bar spacing is designed to prevent escapes for many small species, but always match spacing to your pet's size to avoid injury. Metal bars resist chewing, but inspect for rust or loose joints periodically and address any sharp edges right away.
