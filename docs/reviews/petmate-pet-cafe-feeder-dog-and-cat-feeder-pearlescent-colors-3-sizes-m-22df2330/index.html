Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up the Petmate Gravity Feeder?

Setting up the Petmate Gravity Feeder is straightforward. Simply fill the container with your pet's dry food, secure the lid, and place it on the base. The feeder will automatically dispense food as your pet eats.

Is the feeder suitable for wet food?

No, the Petmate Gravity Feeder is designed for dry food only. Wet food can cause blockages and is not recommended for use with this feeder.

How often should I clean the feeder?

It's best to clean the feeder once a week to maintain hygiene. The removable lid and base make it easy to wash with warm soapy water.

Can the feeder be used outdoors?

While the feeder is durable, it is recommended for indoor use to avoid exposure to the elements, which could affect its performance and longevity.
