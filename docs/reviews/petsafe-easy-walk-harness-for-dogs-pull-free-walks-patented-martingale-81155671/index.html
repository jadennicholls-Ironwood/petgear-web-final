To ensure a proper fit, measure your dog's chest and neck according to the sizing guide provided by PetSafe. Adjust the straps for a snug but comfortable fit, allowing two fingers to fit between the harness and your dog's body.

Is this harness suitable for all dog breeds?

How do I clean the harness?
