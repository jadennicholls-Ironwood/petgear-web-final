Will PurrrfectionPet Axolotl Safe Hideouts & Caves fit in a 10 gallon tank?

Yes, the shelter is sized to sit comfortably in most 10 gallon tanks, though placement matters. Position it near the back or side to give axolotls a private corner, and make sure the chosen spot still allows easy access for feeding and maintenance. How do I set up the airstone with my existing pump?

Attach standard airline tubing to the airstone outlet and connect it to a small air pump with adjustable flow. Use a check valve if the pump will sit below the tank to prevent backflow. Adjust flow so bubbles are gentle, avoid strong currents that could stress gills or dislodge substrate.

What is the recommended cleaning routine for this hideout?

Rinse the hideout in tank water during regular water changes and use a soft brush to remove trapped debris from crevices. For deeper cleaning, soak in dechlorinated water and gently scrub, then rinse thoroughly. Never use soap or bleach, and avoid abrasive scrubbing that could damage the finish.

Is the material safe for axolotl skin and gills?

The resin material is non-toxic once rinsed and cured, and the smooth surface reduces snag risk for delicate skin and gills. It is important to rinse new decor thoroughly to remove any manufacturing residue. Monitor your pet after introduction for normal behavior and ensure the airstone does not create harsh flow.
