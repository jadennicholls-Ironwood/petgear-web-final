Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right size for my pet?

Measure your pet's neck circumference and compare it to the sizing chart provided by the manufacturer. Ensure there's enough space for comfort but not too loose to slip off.

Is the collar suitable for all breeds?

The Supet Inflatable Recovery Collars are designed for small to medium-sized dogs and cats. Always check the size chart to ensure compatibility with your pet's breed and size.

How do I clean the collar?

Cleaning is simple; use a damp cloth with mild soap to wipe the collar. Ensure it is fully dry before reinflating and using it again.

Can my pet eat and drink while wearing the collar?

Yes, the design allows pets to eat and drink comfortably. However, always supervise your pet to ensure they are not struggling.

How long can my pet wear the collar?

Pets can wear the collar as long as needed during recovery. Regularly check for signs of discomfort or irritation and adjust as necessary.
