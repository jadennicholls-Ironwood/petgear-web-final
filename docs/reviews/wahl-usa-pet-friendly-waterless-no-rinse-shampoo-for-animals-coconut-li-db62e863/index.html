How often can I use Wahl Dry Pet Shampoos on my pet?

What is the best way to apply Wahl Dry Pet Shampoos?

Simply spray the shampoo onto your pet's fur, avoiding the eyes, and then rub it in with your hands or a towel. Allow it to dry naturally or towel dry for quicker results.
