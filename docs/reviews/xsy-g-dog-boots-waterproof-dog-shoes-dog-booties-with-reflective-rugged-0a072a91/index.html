How do I determine the right size for my dog?

Measure your dog's paw width and length, then compare it to the size chart provided by XSY&G. It's important to ensure a snug fit to prevent the boots from slipping off during walks.

Are these boots suitable for all weather conditions?

While designed for winter, the XSY&G Dog Winter Boots can also be used in rainy or muddy conditions due to their waterproof material. However, they are not intended for extremely hot surfaces.

How should I clean the boots after use?

Yes, some dogs may need an adjustment period. It's recommended to let your dog wear the boots indoors for short periods to get accustomed before heading outside.
