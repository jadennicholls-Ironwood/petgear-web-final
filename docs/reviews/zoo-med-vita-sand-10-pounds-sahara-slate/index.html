Is Zoo Med Sand & Sand Mixes For Desert Lizards safe for all desert lizards?

Yes, this sand is designed to be safe for most desert-dwelling lizards. Its fine texture minimizes the risk of impaction, making it suitable for species that enjoy digging and burrowing.

How should I clean the sand?

Regular spot cleaning is recommended to remove waste and debris. For a thorough clean, replace the sand periodically and rinse the enclosure with a reptile-safe disinfectant.
