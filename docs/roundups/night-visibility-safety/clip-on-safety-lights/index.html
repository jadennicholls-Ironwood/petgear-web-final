Clip-On Safety Lights

Visibility matters: clip-on safety lights make you more noticeable in low-light conditions during walks, runs, and rides. When comparing options, prioritize raw visibility—lumens, beam pattern, and viewing angle—because being seen at distance is the primary safety benefit. Battery life and power options (rechargeable versus replaceable cells) determine how long a light can protect you between charges or swaps. Also consider mounting versatility and retention, weather resistance, light modes (steady and flashing), and the device’s size and weight for comfort and consistent use. Pick a clip-on light that balances bright, wide coverage with reliable battery life so visibility never compromises mobility.
