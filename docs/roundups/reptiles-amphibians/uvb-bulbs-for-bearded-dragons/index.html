UVB Bulbs For Bearded Dragons

Reliable UVB exposure is essential for bearded dragons' calcium metabolism and long-term health. Choosing the right UVB bulb means balancing spectrum, intensity, and coverage to support natural behaviors and prevent metabolic bone disease. When comparing options, prioritize bulbs that deliver a consistent UVB spectrum in the biologically effective 290–320 nm band and maintain adequate intensity across the basking area. Consider fixture compatibility and distance — even the best bulb can't help if positioned incorrectly — and look for even coverage to minimize shadowed zones. Durability, predictable output decline, and compatibility with preferred basking temperatures are practical factors that improve welfare and reduce maintenance hassle.
