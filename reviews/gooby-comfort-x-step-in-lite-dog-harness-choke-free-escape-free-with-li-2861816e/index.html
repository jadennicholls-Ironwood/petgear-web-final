Measure your dog's chest girth accurately and compare it to the size chart provided by Gooby. The harness should fit snugly but not too tight, allowing for two fingers to fit between the harness and your dog's body.

What is the best way to clean the harness?

The harness can be hand-washed with mild detergent and air-dried. This helps maintain its shape and longevity, ensuring it remains fresh and comfortable for your dog.

Is this harness suitable for all-day wear?

Can this harness be used for dogs that pull?
