Frequently Asked Questions

Will GooingTop LED Plant Lights fit my windowsill pots and small plant stands?

The clip and compact base are designed to fit most household surfaces including narrow windowsills and plant stands. Check that your shelf lip is not thinner than the clamp opening. For very thick shelves you can use the lamp on a table edge or a lightweight stand.

How should I position the lamp for seedlings versus mature foliage?

Seedlings do best closer to the light to prevent stretching, generally six to twelve inches depending on brightness. Mature foliage can be a bit farther back for even coverage, and angling the flexible neck helps spread light across multiple leaves
