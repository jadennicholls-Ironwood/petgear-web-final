How often should I replace the leaf litter in my terrarium?

Replace soiled or compacted sections as needed, typically by spot-cleaning weekly and rotating in fresh material over months. Full replacement depends on enclosure conditions, but many keep the litter for several months while removing decomposed patches to maintain cleanliness and airflow.
