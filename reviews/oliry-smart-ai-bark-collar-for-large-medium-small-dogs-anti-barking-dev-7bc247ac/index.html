Frequently Asked Questions

How do I ensure the Oliry Bark Collar fits my dog properly?

To ensure a proper fit, measure your dog's neck and adjust the collar accordingly. The collar should be snug but not too tight, allowing for two fingers to fit between the collar and your dog's neck.

What are the maintenance requirements for the Oliry Bark Collar?

Maintenance is simple; regularly clean the collar with a damp cloth to remove dirt and debris. Ensure the charging port is dry before recharging to maintain battery health.

Is the Oliry Bark Collar safe for all dog breeds?

Yes, the Oliry Bark Collar is designed to be safe for all breeds. However, it's recommended to monitor your dog's reaction during initial use to ensure comfort and effectiveness.

How does the collar's sensitivity adjustment work?

The collar features five sensitivity levels, allowing you to adjust according to your dog's barking intensity. Start with a lower setting and gradually increase if necessary.

Can the Oliry Bark Collar be used in wet conditions?

Yes, the collar is IP67 waterproof, making it suitable for use in wet conditions. However, it's advisable to remove the collar during prolonged water exposure, such as swimming.
