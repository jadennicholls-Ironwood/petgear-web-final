Is Oxyfresh Terrarium Cleaners & Disinfectants safe to use around amphibians and reptiles?

When used exactly as directed, the formula is designed to be safe for use in enclosures after items and surfaces are rinsed or wiped dry. Avoid spraying directly on animals or on live plants that may be sensitive, and allow treated surfaces to air out before returning animals to the area.

Which surfaces can I clean with Oxyfresh Terrarium Cleaners & Disinfectants?

The cleaner works well on glass, acrylic viewing panels, sealed plastics, and hard enclosure decorations. It is not intended for untreated porous wood or substrate, so test a small hidden area first and avoid saturating absorbent materials to prevent damage or residue buildup.

How do I incorporate this cleaner into a regular maintenance routine?

Use the spray for quick spot cleans after waste events and weekly wipe downs to keep surfaces clear and odor levels low. Regular light maintenance with the product reduces the time and effort needed for full deep cleans, and keeps animals less stressed by shorter disturbance periods.

Are there specific safety steps to follow when using this product?

Always use in a ventilated area and avoid direct contact with animals during application. Wear gloves if you have sensitive skin, follow label dilution or usage instructions, and store the bottle out of reach of pets and children to prevent accidental ingestion or misuse.

Is the cleaner easy to transport for shows or temporary setups?
