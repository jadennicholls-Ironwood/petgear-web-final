Frequently Asked Questions

What size animals work best in PawHut Multi-Level Metal Cages and how much space do they need?

The cage is sized for small animals such as dwarf rabbits, chinchillas, ferrets, and guinea pigs that benefit from vertical space. Measure your pet and allow room to hop between platforms and lie stretched out; larger breeds may need a wider floor plan or a single-level setup to meet activity needs.

How difficult is assembly and what tools are required for setup?

Assembly is straightforward but can take 30 to 60 minutes depending on experience, and having a second person speeds alignment of panels. Basic tools like a screwdriver and adjustable wrench are typically all you need, and the manual shows panel order and latch placement to reduce setup friction.

What is the best way to clean the removable tray and metal bars?

Pull the tray out for daily spot cleaning and use a mild pet-safe detergent for weekly washing, then air dry before reassembly to prevent rust. Wipe the metal bars with a damp cloth and mild cleaner, avoiding harsh abrasives, and inspect joints for buildup to keep latches operating smoothly.

Are there safety concerns with ramps and platforms in the cage?

Check that ramps have textured surfaces or add non-slip covering to prevent slips, especially for young or senior pets. Ensure platforms are securely fastened and latches are closed, and remove any frayed bedding or edges that could trap claws; regular inspections reduce the risk of injury.
