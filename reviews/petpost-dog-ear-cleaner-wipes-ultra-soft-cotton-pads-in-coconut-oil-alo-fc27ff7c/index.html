Frequently Asked Questions

Are Petpost Pet Ear Cleaners suitable for all dog breeds?

Yes, these ear cleaner wipes are designed to be gentle and effective for all dog breeds. However, for larger breeds, you may need to use multiple wipes per cleaning session.

How often should I use these ear cleaner wipes on my dog?

It's recommended to clean your dog's ears once a week or as needed, depending on their ear health and activity level. Regular cleaning helps prevent infections and wax build-up.

How do I use Petpost Pet Ear Cleaners?

Simply take a wipe and gently clean the outer ear and ear canal entrance. Avoid inserting the wipe too deeply into the ear canal to prevent injury.

Are these wipes safe for puppies?

Yes, the gentle formula is safe for puppies. However, always consult with your veterinarian before introducing new products to your pet's care routine.

Can I use these wipes on other pets?

While designed for dogs, the gentle formula may be suitable for other pets with similar ear cleaning needs. Always check with a veterinarian before use on other animals.
