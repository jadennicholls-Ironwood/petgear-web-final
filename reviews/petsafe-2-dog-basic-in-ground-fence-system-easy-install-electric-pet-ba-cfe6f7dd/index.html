How does the PetSafe Invisible Fence Kits work?

The system uses a transmitter to send a radio signal through a buried wire, creating an invisible boundary. The collar worn by the pet receives the signal and provides a warning beep or static correction when the pet approaches the boundary.

Is the system safe for pets?

Yes, the system is designed to be safe for pets. The static correction is mild and adjustable, ensuring it is effective without causing harm. Proper training is essential to ensure pets understand the boundaries.

What is the range of the PetSafe Invisible Fence Kits?

How long does the collar battery last?

Can the system be used with multiple pets?
