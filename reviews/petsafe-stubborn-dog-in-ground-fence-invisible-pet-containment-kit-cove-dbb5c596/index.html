Frequently Asked Questions

How does the PetSafe Invisible Fence Kits work?

The system uses a buried wire to create an invisible boundary. When your dog approaches the boundary, the collar emits a warning tone followed by a static correction if they continue.

Is the system safe for my dog?

Yes, the static correction is safe and humane. It is designed to be a mild deterrent to prevent your dog from crossing the boundary.

How long does it take to train my dog?

Training typically takes about two weeks. Consistent training sessions and positive reinforcement help your dog understand the boundaries quickly.

Can I expand the coverage area?

Yes, additional wire can be purchased to expand the coverage area beyond the initial 1/3 acre. Ensure to follow the manufacturer's guidelines for expansion.

What maintenance is required for the system?

Regularly check the wire for breaks or damage, and ensure the collar battery is charged. Periodic checks help maintain the system's reliability.
