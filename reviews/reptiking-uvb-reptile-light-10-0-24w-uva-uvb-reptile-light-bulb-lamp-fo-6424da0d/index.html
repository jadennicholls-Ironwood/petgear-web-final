Installation is straightforward. Simply screw the bulb into a compatible fixture that can handle the wattage. Ensure the fixture is securely mounted in the terrarium to provide optimal lighting coverage.

Are these bulbs suitable for all reptiles?

These bulbs are best suited for desert reptiles like bearded dragons that require high UVB exposure. For other types of reptiles, check their specific UVB needs before use.

How often should I replace the ReptiKing UVB Bulbs?

It is recommended to replace these bulbs every 6-12 months to ensure consistent UVB output. Over time, the effectiveness of UVB emission decreases even if the bulb still lights up.

Can these bulbs be used with a dimmer switch?

No, UVB bulbs should not be used with dimmer switches, as this can affect their performance and UVB output. Use a standard on/off switch for best results.

What safety precautions should I take when using these bulbs?
