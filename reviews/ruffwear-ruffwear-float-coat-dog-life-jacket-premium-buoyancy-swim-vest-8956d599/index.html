Frequently Asked Questions

How do I determine the right size for my dog?

Measure your dog's girth around the widest part of their ribcage. Ruffwear Pet Life Jackets provide a sizing chart to help you choose the correct size. Ensure the jacket fits snugly but allows for comfortable movement.

Is this life jacket suitable for all water activities?

How should I clean the life jacket?

Rinse the jacket with fresh water after each use to remove salt, chlorine, or debris. Allow it to air dry completely before storing it to prevent mildew and odors.

Can the life jacket be used for puppies?

Yes, as long as the puppy fits within the size range and weight capacity of the jacket. Ensure it is adjusted properly for a secure and comfortable fit.
