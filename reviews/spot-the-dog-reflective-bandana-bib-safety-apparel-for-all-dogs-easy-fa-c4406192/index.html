Frequently Asked Questions

How do I ensure the bandana fits my dog properly?

Measure your dog's neck circumference and compare it to the bandana's size chart. The bandana should fit snugly but not too tight, allowing two fingers to fit comfortably between the bandana and your dog's neck.

Can I wash the bandana in a washing machine?

Yes, the bandana is machine washable. Use a gentle cycle with cold water to maintain its reflective properties and prevent color fading.

Is the bandana safe for nighttime use?

Yes, the reflective material enhances visibility in low-light conditions, making it safer for nighttime walks. However, always supervise your pet and use additional lighting if necessary.

What materials are used in the bandana?

The bandana is made from durable, lightweight fabric with reflective strips designed to withstand regular wear and washing. This ensures long-lasting visibility and comfort for your pet.

Can the bandana be used for other pets?

While designed for dogs, the bandana can be used for other pets of similar size, such as goats or large cats. Ensure the fit is appropriate and comfortable for the animal.
