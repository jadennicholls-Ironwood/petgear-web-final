Frequently Asked Questions

Is Ultum Nature Systems Aquascape Hardscape suitable for saltwater tanks?

Yes, the driftwood can be used in both freshwater and saltwater aquariums. However, it is important to monitor water parameters as natural wood can slightly alter them over time.

How do I prepare the driftwood before adding it to my aquarium?

Rinse the driftwood thoroughly to remove any dust or debris. Soaking it in water for a few days can help it sink more easily and reduce the release of tannins.

Can the driftwood affect the pH levels in my tank?

Natural driftwood can slightly lower pH levels due to the release of tannins. Regular water changes and monitoring can help maintain stable water conditions.

Is the driftwood safe for all aquatic life?

Yes, the driftwood is safe for fish and invertebrates. Its natural composition makes it a suitable addition to most aquascapes, providing hiding spots and surfaces for beneficial bacteria.

How long does the driftwood last in an aquarium?

The driftwood is durable and can last for several years in an aquarium. Its longevity depends on water conditions and maintenance practices.
