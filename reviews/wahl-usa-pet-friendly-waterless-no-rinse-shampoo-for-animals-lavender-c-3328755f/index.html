How do I apply Wahl Dry Pet Shampoos?

Simply spray the shampoo onto your pet's coat, avoiding the eyes and ears, and then towel dry. The formula is designed to be left on without rinsing, making it easy to use anywhere.

Is this shampoo suitable for all dog breeds?

Yes, Wahl Dry Pet Shampoos are formulated for use on all dog breeds. However, always perform a patch test on a small area first to ensure there is no adverse reaction.

How often can I use this shampoo on my pet?
