Reptile Hides & Caves

Many reptiles and amphibians rely on well-designed hides to reduce stress and promote natural behaviors. When comparing hides and caves, focus on security, appropriate size, material safety, and the ability to support thermal and humidity needs. Select hides that offer security, proper thermal gradients, and humidity retention for healthier behavior. Durability and easy-to-clean surfaces reduce maintenance while naturalistic textures or hollow logs can encourage hiding and ambushing behaviors.
