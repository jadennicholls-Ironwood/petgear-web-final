UV/Sun Shirts for Dogs

Protecting a dog's skin from UV exposure reduces sunburn risk and helps prevent long-term damage, so specialized UV/sun shirts are an essential layer for sunny days. Choosing the right sun shirt centers on three things: fabric UPF, a secure but comfortable fit, and coverage for high-exposure areas like the ears, nose, and belly. Look for breathable, quick-dry materials that block UVA/UVB while allowing airflow to prevent overheating. Stretch and adjustable closures help maintain coverage during walks and play, keeping dogs comfortable without restriction. Additional features such as reflective trim, machine-washable construction, and easy sizing make daily use practical for active households.
